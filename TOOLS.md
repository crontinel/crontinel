## Product heuristics

- Keep MVP scope ruthlessly small, only must-haves first.
- Keep the landing experience clean, fast, and convincing.
- Treat launch as the start of the work, distribution matters heavily after launch.
- Talk directly to users often, DM/email/call when useful.
- Prioritize retention, not just acquisition.
- Dogfood the product regularly to catch real issues early.
- Price on value, not by copying competitors.
- Respect unsubscribes and opt-outs as honest feedback.
- Aim for meaningful upside, not tiny ceilings.
- Keep kill criteria in mind if a path is clearly not working.
- Offer convenient social login where it materially reduces signup friction.
