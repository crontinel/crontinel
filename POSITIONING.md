# Crontinel Positioning

Last updated: 2026-04-10

---

## 1. One-Line Positioning

Crontinel is cloud cron monitoring for everyone. Know when your cron jobs stop running, before your customers do.

---

## 2. Tagline

"Your cron jobs have been silently broken for 3 days."

---

## 3. Elevator Pitch

Your uptime monitor shows green. Your cron jobs have been failing since Tuesday. Crontinel monitors what generic tools miss: whether your scheduled tasks actually ran, whether queues are backed up, whether workers are alive. Alerts in minutes, not days.

---

## 4. Who It's For

| Segment | Why they need it |
|---|---|
| Developers with scheduled tasks | Every app has cron jobs. Nobody watches them. One failure silently breaks things for days. |
| SaaS operators | Your product runs on background jobs. When they stop, customers stop getting invoices, notifications, reports. |
| DevOps/SRE teams | Get visibility into the jobs and schedulers running across your infrastructure, not just HTTP responses. |
| Agencies | Monitor every client's cron jobs from one dashboard without logging into servers. |

---

## 5. What Makes It Different

Generic uptime tools check if a URL returns 200. Crontinel checks if your jobs actually ran, if your queues are healthy, if your workers are alive. It reads the actual state of your system, not just surface-level pings.
